What is Amazon Echo Spot?

Echo Spot is developed by Amazon, it is a smart alarm clock that is controlled by voice commands. Echo Spot is mainly designed to minimize the dependency of alarm clocks in smartphones. It also provides a number of different features to the user such as:

  • Integrated camera for video calls
  • Using voice commands , Echo Spot can play music, videos, reminders, answer questions, integrate and communicate with other smart home gadgets.
  • Its touch screen displays information such as trending news, shopping list, to-do lists and weather forecast. The theme and backgrounds on the screen are also customizable

Give it a Try!

The Echo Spot assistant is called Alexa, for Alexa to respond you must call her name ! Say Alexa , and ask her to set an alarm , or reminder, or even tell you a joke! If you wish to know more about what Alexa can do for you just say "Alexa, Give me a tip".
